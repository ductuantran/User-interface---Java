The codes are for implementing a PhotoViewer.

The functions implemented are :

- Open images to window, it is resized to fit the frame.

- The image and panel are resized when the frame is dragged.

- Flip mode is implemented, double-click to see the back of the image.

- Able to draw strokes on the back of photo.

- Able to choose color with a color chooser.

- Able to choose the pen's thickness with a slider.

- When clicking the back of photo, the "text typing mode" is activated.
The keys pressed are captured by the program and shown on the console, 
but not shown on the image yet.
